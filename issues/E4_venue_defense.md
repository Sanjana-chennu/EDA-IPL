## E4: Venue Chasing vs. Defending Paradise (Medium)

Some stadiums have short boundaries; others have evening dew that makes the ball hard to grip for bowlers in the second innings. Which venues heavily favor the team batting second?

### The Objective
Determine the statistical advantage of batting first vs. second for different stadiums.

### Requirements:
1. Identify the top 10 venues by total matches hosted using `matches.csv`.
2. For these venues, calculate the ratio of matches won by the team batting first vs. the team batting second (chasing).
3. **(New)** Split your calculation into two eras: "Early IPL" (seasons before 2015) and "Modern IPL" (2015 onwards). Calculate if the defending vs chasing advantage flipped across these eras for the top 10 stadiums.
4. **Visualization:** Generate a **grouped bar plot** or **heatmap** showing the win ratio for defending/chasing across the two distinct eras for these top venues.

### Mandatory Submission Rule:
Submit your solution via a Pull Request. In your PR description, include a screenshot of your generated visualisation, a copy of the core logic code cell, and a **Markdown** summary. Reference your visual—which stadium would you want to chase a total at, and which stadium is a fortress for defending teams?
